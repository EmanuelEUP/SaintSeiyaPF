SAINT SEIYA

715/5000 Saint Seiya (聖 闘 士 星矢 Seinto Seiya), literally in Spanish Seiya El Santo and known as The Knights of the Zodiac, is a manga series written and illustrated by Masami Kurumada. It was published since December 3, 1985 in the magazine Shūkan Shōnen Jump from the Shūeisha publishing house until December 12, 1990 in V Jump magazine. 9 It was later adapted into a 114-episode animated series (anime), a 31-episode OVA and a 13-episode ONA, in total 158. Five animated films and one CG animated film have also been produced, in addition to several official prequels and sequels to the original story.

108 chapters of the manga were published along with a special edition, making a total of 109 in tankoubon format, divided into 28 volumes with three main sagas (story arcs): Sanctuary, Poseidon and Hades. It has been reprinted and reissued in Wideban, Bunkoban and Kanzenban formats and also the so-called "Remix", the production in three editions (2002, 2007 and 2012).

After the anime adaptation, two more series have been produced: the manga titled Saint Seiya Lost Canvas by TMS Entertainment and Saint Seiya Omega by Toei Animation. Contrary to Saint Seiya Lost Canvas, Saint Seiya Omega was animated and it was not until after an adaptation to the manga was made, although curiously both series are spin-offs. A third anime produced by Toei Animation, Saint Seiya Soul of Gold whose broadcast began on April 11, 2015, the series recounts the events after the destruction of the Wailing Wall, where Aioria de Leo is the protagonist and wakes up in the middle of Asgard along with the other golden knights. At present Saint Seiya is still in force with the Saint Seiya Next Dimension manga, the official continuation of the series whose animated version has been confirmed for the future, and with Saint Seiya Saintia Sho. THE SERIE

The series focuses on a group of young warriors called "knights" (or "saints"), whose main protagonist is Seiya (knight wearing Pegasus armor). These warriors fight on the side of the Greek goddess Athena reincarnated in the human Saori Kido to protect humanity from the evil forces that want to dominate Earth. For their battles, each knight uses her inner energy bound to the universe (the "cosmos"), her special fighting techniques, and her. armor (originally called cloth), all inspired by each of the 88 constellations.

Both the original manga and the anime adaptation had great success in Japan, as well as in European countries such as France, Italy, Spain, Portugal, and in most Latin American countries. However, the anime It was not translated into English until her arrival in the United States in 2003.

In 2016, for the thirtieth anniversary of the classic Saint Seiya manga by Masami Kurumada, a commemorative exhibition was held in Japan with many products related to the classic Saint Seiya manga (Masami Kurumada) and the classic Saint Seiya anime (Toei Animation). This great commemorative exhibition was also held in China in Hong Kong. 15

THE 12 HOUSES

The battle begins when the knights and Athena are looking for the Patriarch since Saori announced that they would soon go to the Sanctuary to see him. A hooded figure appears and guides them to where the Patriarch is; explains that they must go through the 12 houses of the Zodiac to be able to see it (which nobody has achieved). Suddenly, this person reveals himself as Ptolemy of the Arrow, and prevents them from continuing, saying that he will not let them even reach Aries, the first House. Ptolemy attacks them with arrows, but Seiya is able to take him down easily with his meteor attack. Everything seems to have gone well; however, they realize that Athena was wounded with a golden arrow to her chest. Ptolemy He tells them that the only one who can withdraw the arrow is the Patriarch and when the arrow was stuck, a twelve-hour cycle began that at the end of the arrow would pierce his heart completely and they only have that time to pierce the 12 houses.

At the end of the long battle, Athena returns to normality and decides to put things in order while Saga commits suicide with the scepter of Athena repentant of all his acts and makes the evil that caused the double personality disappear so that die like the noble knight that she was, Athena cries apologizing to Seiya and the other knights for the suffering that they went through in the battle of the twelve houses.

MUSICALS

In August 1991, under the patronage of Bandai, a Saint Seiya musical was performed at the Aoyama Theater in Tokyo, Japan. The story told the sagas of the Sanctuary and of Poseidon. The cast included SMAP members like the Five Bronze Saints and Poseidon. The characters of Mu de Aries, Aioria de Leo and Milo de Scorpio were played by members of another band: Tokio.
