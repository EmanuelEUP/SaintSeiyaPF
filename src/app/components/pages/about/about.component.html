ABOUT

This website built with angular technology, mainly corresponds to a final project for the approval and certification of the course taught by escalab, corresponding to Angular 8.

The final project of the course will be free knowledge, where students can carry out an idea of interest, based on the knowledge acquired throughout the course.

INSTRUCTIONS

  • - The project will be evaluated based on a guideline initially defined by the instructor and communicated in advance for the students.
  • - The student will have a maximum month for the development and delivery on Github of their final project.
  • - Instructor assistance during the execution and development of the project by Slack and Email.
  • - The project is delivered in a Github repository to be evaluated and subsequently certified.
  • - The student must meet a minimum of 75% attendance to pass the course.
